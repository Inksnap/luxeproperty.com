General Questions

What services does LuxeProperties offer?

LuxeProperties offers comprehensive real estate services including property listings, buyer and seller representation, property valuations, market analysis, and expert consultation from our team of experienced agents.

How do I search for properties?

You can search for properties using our search filters on the Properties page. Filter by location, property type, price range, number of bedrooms, and more. You can also save your favorite properties by creating a free account.

Is it free to use LuxeProperties?

Yes, browsing properties and creating an account is completely free. There are no fees for buyers to use our services. Our agents are compensated by sellers when a property is sold.

Buying a Property

How do I schedule a property viewing?

You can schedule a viewing by clicking the "Schedule Tour" button on any property listing page, or by contacting the listing agent directly. You can also contact us through our contact form or by phone.

Do I need to get pre-approved for a mortgage?

While not required to browse properties, getting pre-approved for a mortgage is highly recommended before making an offer. It shows sellers you're a serious buyer and helps you understand your budget.

Selling a Property

How do I list my property for sale?

To list your property, contact one of our experienced agents through our Agents page or contact form. Our agents will provide a free property valuation and guide you through the listing process.

What are the commission rates?

Commission rates vary depending on the property type, location, and services provided. Our agents will discuss commission rates and services during your initial consultation. Contact us for more information.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help. Contact us today!
